Sealos gives you the full power of a cloud platform without the complexity. Whether you're spinning up a development environment, deploying a production app, managing databases, or building AI-powered services, Sealos handles the Kubernetes complexity so you can focus on what matters: shipping software.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up or self-host">
    Use the managed cloud at [sealos.io](https://os.sealos.io) or [self-host Sealos](/self-hosting/overview) on your own infrastructure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a workspace">
    Your workspace is your isolated cloud environment. Invite teammates, set resource quotas, and manage billing from one place. See [Workspaces](/platform/workspaces).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy your first app">
    Open **App Launchpad**, enter your Docker image, and click deploy. Sealos handles TLS, ingress, and auto-scaling. See the [Quick Start](/quickstart) for a full walkthrough.
  </Step>
</Steps>
